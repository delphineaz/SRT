Scheduler¶

The antenna is not in tracking when the @ is red. Note that it can also be red when the antenna is in slewing (to reach the position of a target).

When the status of the scheduler is in FAILURE and the scan/subscan number is frozen, stop the schedule.

MeteoClient¶

The real-time monitoring of the wind velocity is performed with the meteoClient on a nuraghe-mng shell: $ meteoClient. If the wind speed exceeds 60 km/h, the antenna must be stowed immediately.

Fig.9 The wind speed exceeds 60 km/h, as indicated with the red horizontal line.
